When we travel east or west through timezones, we don't gain or lose any time. 

Time only matters to the person observing it. 
Timezone is a standard enforced across the world to aid our daily activites. 
Your life(time) does not depend on man made concepts. 

If you have that kind of mindset, you can now release yourself from it.
